Uno

An all-favourite classic attraction is now available in online format! Uno is an attraction that can unite a whole family in one arena, so don’t hesitate to check it out! This version presents a renovated and refined set of rules with a bunch of new aspects that bring additional spice to the riveting competitions.

How to Play Uno

The rules of this version don’t differ from the original game, but a very convenient interface together with diverse regimes present a totally new level of involvement. Ask your family of friends to make you a company and enjoy thousands of rounds filled with joy of victory or bitterness of defeat.

  • Receive valuable treasures. Performing well in Uno game is always rewarded with all sorts of bonuses and boosters. From additional cards to the currency that allows you to acquire special tools – obtain all possible prizes!
  • Pick up exclusive modes. The game encourages you to get out of your comfort zone and try something new. Take a look at different variations of Uno and choose the most amusing one with your companions.
  • Alter the standards. In case you just want to adapt several aspects to your style, you may totally do that in the separate settings. Make the rules stricter and add a little bit of challenge, or vice versa, play an easy round to relax.
  • Celebrate your triumph. Each victory makes you closer to the Olympus of the world-wide ratings. Outsmart the greatest number of opponents and learn to use any benefits you have to achieve such heights.

Uno Multiverse is Waiting for You!

Apart from custom plays, the game also includes lots of thematic events and other special rounds you can play to get rare rewards. The realm of Uno is full of unpredictable things that can turn out to be pleasant surprises of hidden trials that will make you question your own abilities, diversifying your experience.

Play in Pairs

Collaborate with your buddy and form an undefeatable alliance in this wonderful game, competing against another team of enthusiasts! Get two times more cards and opportunities, following your friend’s devastating punches with your no less smashing moves.

And if you want something extreme, venture for the wildest regime the game grants. In this case, the results are absolutely unpredictable, leaving you with a constant feeling of anticipation. Win everything at once or end the session without a penny in your pocket – it all depends on pure luck!
